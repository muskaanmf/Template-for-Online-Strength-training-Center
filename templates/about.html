Our Mission

Strength and Conditioning (S&C) is the selection and development of dynamic /static exercises used to improve physical performance. Whilst it originally benefited athletes it is now widely used in both the sporting world and more generally.

Our Vision

S&C is used to develop every area of the body and improve the way a person moves, with the intention of enhancing sporting or physical performance. Research demonstrates that correct and appropriate training can improve physical performance.
